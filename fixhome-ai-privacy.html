FIXHOME AI

AI-Powered Malfunction Diagnosis & Smart Home Assistant

App Description

FIXHOME AI is a smart assistant application designed to help users quickly identify and troubleshoot home appliance malfunctions and tech issues using the power of Google Vertex AI (Gemini). It provides real-time chat, photo analysis, and device management capabilities securely and efficiently.

PRIVACY POLICY

1. Data Storage and Local Storage

FixHome AI prioritizes user privacy. Your saved devices, model info, and chat history with AI are stored entirely in your device's local memory. This data is not transferred to or stored in any central database on our side.

2. Authentication and Account Data

When using the app with 'Guest Login,' no personal data is collected. If you sign in with Google, your email and profile name are processed via secure Firebase infrastructure only to manage your session and create a profile for you.

3. AI Operations

Google Vertex AI (Gemini) is used for malfunction diagnoses. Text-based questions and photos taken for analysis are processed only to generate the current solution. This data is not stored by us.

4. Ads and Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Ad services may use your device's anonymous advertising ID to provide more relevant ads.

5. Deleting Data

If you delete the app, all local data (devices and chats) stored on your phone is automatically deleted.
